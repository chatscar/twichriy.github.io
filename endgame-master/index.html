Loop Hero!

In Loop Hero, players control a hero who must explore a procedurally generated world in order to defeat the Lich. The world is composed of a loop of tiles, and each tile can be either a monster, a resource, or an event. As the hero explores the loop, they will collect cards, which can be used to build structures, recruit allies, or upgrade the hero's stats. The goal of the game is to defeat the Lich before the loop resets.
